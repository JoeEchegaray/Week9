Some of my Favorite Video Games!

I have Always Enjoyed playing Video Games in my free time. While I don't have a lot of time to play video games anymore, I still love playing them when I have a chance to. This Website showcases some of my favorite Video Games

Super Mario RPG

1996, Super Nintendo Entertainment System

Play along with Mario and his friends as they seek to defeat Smithy, who has crashed into their world and stolen the seven star pieces of Star Road.


The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

1998, Nintendo 64

Play with Link in the fantasy land of Hyrule on a quest to stop the evil king Ganondorf, by traveling through time and navigating dungeons and an overworld.


Final Fantasy XIV

2003, Windows/OSX/PlayStation

Play along in this MMORPG to defend Eorzea from the invasion of the Garlean Empire as it rebuilds from the 7th Umbral Calamity


Satisfactory

2019, Windows

Play as a FICSIT Employee who's task is to explore an alien planet, and harness it's resources to build complex factories.
