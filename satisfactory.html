2019, Windows
Coffee Stain Publishing
Photo of Smelters and Miners from Satisfactory Game Play

What the Game is About

Satisfactory is a factory simulation game where you play in a 3D first person open world. Your objective is to explore the alien planet you have landed on and collect the planet's natural resources to construct complex factories for automating your resource needs, unlocking more tools, and advanced resources and recipes as you progress.

Why I love this Game!

I Love playing this game because it is a relaxing open world exploration game where I can create factories, and optomize them as I go. The Relaxing Music and amazing graphics make playing this game feel like I am immersed into the game. Being able to creatively build factories in cool and symmetrical designs is always a satisfactory feeling once the build is completed. If you enjoy playing open world creative games such as MineCraft, Sim City, Sims, etc. You really should check this game out. It's a blast!
