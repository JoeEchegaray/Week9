Super Mario RPG and the Legend of the Seven Stars

1996, Super Nintendo Entertainment System
Developed by Square, Published by Nintendo
Photo from a cutscene during Super Mario RPG Game Play

What the Game is About

Mario sets out to rescue Princess Toadstool, infiltrating the castle to which she has been taken and challenging kidnapper King Bowser. During the battle, a giant living sword named Exor falls from the sky, breaks through the Star Road (a pathway that helps grant people's wishes), and crashes into Bowser's castle,[2] sending Mario, Princess Toadstool, and Bowser flying in different directions, as well as scattering the seven-star fragments.Mario must find his friends and travel the world to find the missing pieces of star road to rebuild the pathway to grant people's wishes. (Source: Wikipedia)

Why I love this Game!

This is the first turn based style RPG that I ever played, and with the music of the game and the style of game play, I immediately fell in love with this style of game play, while there was a set story and path, there was still some freedom to explore about the world as you progressed in the game.
